How to Use Ìkókó

A simple guide to creating, managing and sharing your baby name flashcards.

1

Create a Baby

Go to the Add New page from the navigation bar. Under the Baby section, type a name or label for your baby (e.g. "Adriel") and click Create Baby.

This creates a container that will hold all the name entries and their details.

2

Add Name Entries

Once you create or select a baby, the Add Name Entry form appears. Fill in the following fields:

  • Name — The name you want to add (required).
  • Phonetic — How the name is pronounced, e.g. /ˈleɪ.əl/ (optional).
  • Meaning — The meaning or significance of the name (required).
  • Passage — A biblical or islamic or cultural reference, e.g. Numbers 3:24 (optional).
  • Sort Order — A number to control the display order of names (defaults to 0).

Click Add Name to save the entry. You can add as many names as you like under each baby.

3

Record and Upload Pronunciation

After adding a name, it will appear in the Names Added list. Each name has an Audio button next to it.

  1. Record the pronunciation of the name on your phone or computer. Save it as an .mp3, .m4a, .wav, or .ogg file.
  2. Click the Audio button next to the name entry.
  3. Select your audio file from your device.
  4. The file will upload automatically. You will see a ♫ Audio attached confirmation once it is done.

When viewing the flashcard, a speaker icon will appear on the card. Click it to hear the pronunciation.

4

View Flashcards

There are two ways to view your flashcards:

  • From the Dashboard, click on any baby card to open its flashcards.
  • From the Add New page, click the View Flashcards link at the bottom after selecting or creating a baby.

On the view page:

  • Tap the card to flip it and see the meaning on the back.
  • Use the small arrows and progress bar at the bottom of the card to navigate between name entries.
  • Use the large arrows on the left and right to switch between different babies (if you have more than one).
  • Click Download to save the card as an image.
5

Share with Friends and Family

To share your baby name flashcards with someone:

  1. Open the flashcard view page for the baby you want to share.
  2. Copy the URL from your browser's address bar (it will look like /view.html?id=123).
  3. Send the link to your friend or family member.

When they open the link, they will be asked to register and log in if they don't have an account. Once logged in, they will see the flashcards in view-only mode with a banner showing your name as the creator.

6

Edit and Delete

  • To rename a baby, click the pencil icon on the baby card in the Dashboard.
  • To delete a baby and all its names, click the X icon on the baby card. You will be asked to confirm.
  • To delete a name entry, go to the Add New page, select the baby, and click Delete next to the name.
7

Dark Mode

Click the moon/sun icon in the navigation bar to toggle between light and dark themes. Your preference is saved automatically.
